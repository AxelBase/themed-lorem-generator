Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 9, 2025

Welcome to the AxelBase Themed Dummy Text Generator, a completely client-side web utility that generates themed placeholder text (Hipster, Zombie, Cat, Yoda, and Corporate) directly in your browser. We are deeply committed to your privacy and have designed this tool from the ground up to collect absolutely no personal data whatsoever.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any information about you or your usage. Every word generated — whether it's "brains", "purr", or "synergy" — is created instantly on your device and never leaves it.

No Data Collection

This tool operates entirely in your browser using JavaScript. When you select a theme and click "Generate", all processing happens locally. We do not:

  • Track your IP address or location
  • Log which themes you prefer
  • Use cookies, localStorage, or any persistent tracking
  • Employ analytics tools (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.)
  • Load third-party scripts that monitor behavior

How Your Privacy Is Protected

The generator runs 100% offline-capable after the initial page load. You can even disconnect from the internet and continue generating themed text. All themes and word pools are embedded directly in the code — no external requests are made during generation.

Your generated text — no matter how many paragraphs of Zombie apocalypse or Cat zoomies — stays exclusively on your device.

Third-Party Services

We use only trusted CDNs (Bootstrap, Google Fonts) to deliver styling and fonts. These services may set minimal technical cookies for performance, but we do not use any marketing, advertising, or analytics services.

Security & Responsibility

Since no data is transmitted, there is no server to breach. Your privacy is protected by design, not by policy alone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new features are added. Any changes will be clearly dated at the top of this page.

Your privacy is not negotiable. This tool exists to help designers and developers — not to harvest data. You can use it with complete confidence.
